This BaseNotifications is responsible for delegating work between objects: 

 * The createBigTextStyleNotification method receives requests from:
-	BaseNotifications
-	DeviceNotifications
-	WearNotifications

 * and delegate the request to: 
-	Account
-	NotificationHolder



 * The createAndInitializeNotificationBuilder method receives requests from:
-	BaseNotifications
-	DeviceNotifications

 * and delegate the request to: 
-	Account
