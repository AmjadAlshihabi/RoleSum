This SearchAccount is responsible for delegating work between objects: 

 * The createUnifiedInboxAccount method receives requests from:
-	SearchAccount
-	MessageProvider
-	MessagesQueryHandler
-	MessageListWidgetProvider
-	AccountList

 * and delegate the request to: 
-	LocalSearch



 * The getRelatedSearch method receives requests from:
-	SearchAccount
-	MessageProvider
-	MessagesQueryHandler
-	MessageListWidgetProvider

 * and delegate the request to: 
-	LocalSearch
