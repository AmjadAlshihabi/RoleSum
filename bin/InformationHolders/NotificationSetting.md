* vibrateTimes
* vibrateEnabled
* ledColor
* ringtoneUri
* ringEnabled
* vibratePattern
* ledEnabled

* [Account](../InformationHolders/Account.md) has a role type of Information Holder
* [DeviceNotifications](../ServiceProviders/DeviceNotifications.md) has a role type of Service Provider
